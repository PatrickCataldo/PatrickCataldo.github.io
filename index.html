CSV Automation Tool July 2023 - August 2023

Description:
During my internship at Junior Golf Scoreboard, I developed an excel-based application that allowed the team to speed up data transformation and upload processes.
Problem:
Junior Golf Scoreboard brings an intern to their team every summer to help combat the volumn of tournaments sent in by clients to their organization. Their previous process of handling data correction was to manually correct each cell that had missing or invalid values, which slowed tournament upload time.
Action:
To help streamline the process of downloading tournament data, correcting data, and exporting it as a csv file for upload, I built a tool in excel to do the work for them. I created several versions, each one improving on the last. I added several features like admin controls, a data preview tool, and a form clear button.
Result:
After my tool was integrated into Junior Golf Scoreboards business process, there was a significant reduction in both uploaded errors, and time taken to upload data.
